01 Built for

Every game.

MODE//SIM

Sims & cockpits

Check your six in DCS. Look into the apex in iRacing. Scan instruments in MSFS, mirrors in ETS2. Native OpenTrack support, straight from your phone.

MODE//WRLD

Open worlds & adventures

Subnautica's depths, Outer Wilds' skies, Skyrim at eye level — single-player worlds feel different when you can actually look around in them. Free community mods make it happen.

MODE//LIVE

Streaming

A face cam that moves with you — lean in, look around, react. Plus greenscreen, audio, and lighting built in. One app replaces a webcam and a pile of OBS filters.

MODE//ALL

…and everything else.

Games without native tracking support can get it through free, open-source community mods — shared on GitHub and Nexus Mods, easy to install and just as easy to remove. 30+ games and counting, with new releases regularly. Lopari launches any of them with tracking enabled in one click. The mods are free and always will be.

02 Capability

What it does.

6Degrees of freedom
60FPS tracking
1080p60Video to OBS
0Extra hardware
Tracking // 01

Full 6DOF at 60fps, via TrueDepth on iPhone, Accela-filtered on-device. Smooth before it ever leaves your phone. Direct to any OpenTrack UDP game, or route through OpenTrack for custom curves and other protocols. Per-axis sensitivity from 0.1x to 5.0x.

Streaming // 02

1080p60 video and noise-cancelled audio straight into OBS via QuickFeed — our ultra low latency transport. Chroma key, background blur, and face lighting built in. One app, one source.

Chroma Key & Blur

Greenscreen and background blur, no extra software.

Face Lighting

Your screen as a fill light with adjustable warmth.

Per-Axis Tuning

Each axis 0.1x–5.0x. Dial in your sensitivity.

04 Get started

Preflight.

Phone QuickFeed · UDP :4242 OBS + Game
01

Mount your phone

Clip it to your monitor, prop it on your desk — face the front camera toward you.

02

Connect

Same WiFi network, or plug in via USB with the companion app for a wired connection.

03

Go

Install the OBS plugin, and your video feed appears as a source. Tracking data flows straight to OpenTrack UDP games, or route through OpenTrack for others. No native support? Grab the mod.
