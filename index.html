What it does

Head Tracking

6DOF via TrueDepth at 60fps. Accela-filtered on-device to kill jitter before it ever hits the network.

NDI Streaming

Near-instant 1080p60 video to OBS. Auto-discovered on your network, just pick it from a list.

OpenTrack Compatible UDP

Sends tracking data to any OpenTrack-compatible game. Route through OpenTrack itself for additional smoothing and curve tuning.

Chroma Key & Blur

Virtual greenscreen and background blur. No extra software needed.

Face Lighting

Face illumination with adjustable color temperature and brightness.

Audio Capture

Stream audio alongside video with built-in noise cancellation.

Axis Sensitivity

Per-axis tuning from 0.1x to 5.0x. Dial in exactly the feel you want.
