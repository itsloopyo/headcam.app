What it does
Tracking

Full 6DOF at 60fps via TrueDepth, Accela-filtered on-device. Smooth before it ever leaves your phone. Direct to any OpenTrack UDP game, or route through OpenTrack for custom curves and other protocols. Per-axis sensitivity from 0.1x to 5.0x.

Streaming

1080p60 video and noise-cancelled audio straight into OBS via QuickFeed — our ultra low latency transport. Chroma key, background blur, and face lighting built in. One app, one source.

Chroma Key & Blur

Greenscreen and background blur, no extra software.

Face Lighting

Your screen as a fill light with adjustable warmth.

Per-Axis Tuning

Each axis 0.1x–5.0x. Dial in your sensitivity.

Get started

Mount your iPhone

Clip it to your monitor, prop it on your desk — face the front camera toward you.

Connect

Same WiFi network, or plug in via USB with the companion app for a wired connection.

Go

Install the OBS plugin, and your video feed appears as a source. Tracking data flows straight to OpenTrack UDP games, or route through OpenTrack for others.

Works with

  • OBS Studio
  • OpenTrack
  • Any OpenTrack UDP game (direct)
  • Other protocols via OpenTrack relay

Requirements

  • iPhone X or later
  • iOS 14+
  • WiFi or USB cable

Protocols

  • QuickFeed
  • OpenTrack UDP :4242
