What it does
01

Head Tracking

Full 6DOF at 60fps via TrueDepth. Accela-filtered on-device — silky smooth before it ever hits the network.

02

QuickFeed to OBS

1080p60 video straight to OBS via our ultra low latency transport. Install the plugin, your feed appears.

03

OpenTrack UDP

Plug-and-play with any game that accepts OpenTrack UDP. For games that need other protocols, route through OpenTrack for mapping, smoothing, and custom curves.

04

Chroma Key & Blur

Full greenscreen and background blur with no extra software. Stream-ready backgrounds out of the box.

05

Face Lighting

Turn your screen into a fill light. Dial in color temperature and brightness for a clean, well-lit look.

06

Audio Capture

Mic audio synced to your video feed with noise cancellation baked in. One source, stream-ready.

07

Per-Axis Tuning

Tune each axis independently from 0.1x to 5.0x. Dial in exactly how your movement maps to the game.

Get started

Mount your iPhone

Clip it to your monitor, prop it on your desk — face the front camera toward you.

Connect

Same WiFi network, or plug in via USB with the companion app for a wired connection.

Go

Install the OBS plugin, and your video feed appears as a source. Tracking data flows straight to OpenTrack UDP games, or route through OpenTrack for others.

Works with

  • OBS Studio
  • OpenTrack
  • Any OpenTrack UDP game (direct)
  • Other protocols via OpenTrack relay

Requirements

  • iPhone X or later
  • iOS 14+
  • WiFi or USB cable

Protocols

  • QuickFeed
  • OpenTrack UDP :4242
